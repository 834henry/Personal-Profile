About Me

I'm Henry Messiah TMT, a clinical pharmacist who has successfully transitioned into technical writing and software development. With a Master's degree in Clinical Pharmacy from Niger Delta University and years of experience in healthcare, I've developed sharp analytical skills, attention to detail, and the ability to translate complex technical concepts into clear, actionable documentation. My unique healthcare-to-tech journey has positioned me as a specialized technical writer focusing on developer tools, testing automation, and blockchain technology. I create comprehensive tutorials and documentation for platforms like LambdaTest, with published work featured in PHP Architect, DEV Community, Medium, and In Plain English. My technical expertise spans JavaScript/TypeScript, React, Node.js, PHP, Solidity, and blockchain development, with a particular interest in health-tech applications, Web3 technologies, and developer tooling. I recently authored an extensive Solidity to Compact migration guide for Midnight Network and completed a dApp migration project. At my core, I'm driven by curiosity, continuous learning, and the desire to bridge the gap between complex technology and practical understanding. Whether writing technical documentation or building applications, I thrive at the intersection of clarity, precision, and innovation.
